Piña Palmera

For more than 40 years, the Zipolite nonprofit, Piña Palmera, has been part of a collective effort on the Oaxaca coast to promote inclusion and defend the rights of people with disabilities. They work hand in hand with local Indigenous and rural communities, valuing their knowledge, priorities, and experiences.

Since its founding, Piña Palmera A.C. has been dedicated to community rehabilitation. Its work covers essential areas such as medical care, education, social integration, and empowerment, always seeking to foster self-sufficiency, community inclusion, and respect.
