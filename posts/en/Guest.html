Personal retreats

Self-directed personal retreats are opportunities for an individual, couple, family, or small group to grow your relationship with yourself, each other, and the plant and animal communities of the Costa
[wa·HA·ka]
Oaxaca
and Sierra Madre del Sur region.
This could be a meditation, a program that incorporates reading and study, bodywork, dancing, journaling, fasting, or other practices, recreation, and pastimes.
